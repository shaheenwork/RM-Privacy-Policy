1 Introduction

Random Malayali ("the App," "we," "our," or "us") is an anonymous chat application available on Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices available to you when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2 No Account Required

Random Malayali does not require you to create an account, register, or log in. You can use the App anonymously without providing any personal credentials.

3 Information We Do Not Collect ✓ None

We do not collect, store, or process any of the following personally identifiable information:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Home, mailing, or billing address
  • Date of birth or age
  • Government-issued identification
  • Contacts, social media accounts, or linked profiles
  • Payment or financial information

5 IP Address and Abuse Reports

To maintain a safe community, the App may record the public IP address of a user only when another user submits an abuse report — for spam, harassment, inappropriate content, or violations of our community guidelines.

The IP address collected in this context is used exclusively for:

  • Investigating reported violations
  • Preventing spam, fraud, and misuse
  • Enforcing our community guidelines and terms of service

IP addresses are not used for advertising, user profiling, or any purpose other than moderation.

6 Anonymous Chat

Conversations within Random Malayali are anonymous. Chats are not linked to personal identities, user accounts, or any identifying information. We do not store the content of your conversations on our servers beyond what is required for real-time message delivery.

Information processed in connection with the App is used solely to:

  • Deliver push notifications
  • Display advertisements through Google AdMob

  • Prevent spam, fraud, and violations of our community guidelines

9 Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to any third parties.

Information may be disclosed only in the following limited circumstances:

We retain information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable law. IP addresses collected for abuse moderation are retained for a limited period and deleted when no longer needed for the relevant investigation.
